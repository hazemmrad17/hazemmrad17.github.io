Project

Club Africain

A heartfelt graphic design project dedicated to the legacy of Club Africain, created for a summer internship application.

The Club Africain project is a deeply personal endeavor, blending graphic design with a tribute to the iconic Tunisian football club founded in 1920. As the son of a former active member and passionate supporter, I crafted this project to honor my father's legacy and my lifelong connection to the club. Created as part of my application for a summer graphic design internship, it showcases my creative skills and passion for the red and white heritage. Below, explore the project details, including historical imagery and design concepts that bridge the club's past and future.
