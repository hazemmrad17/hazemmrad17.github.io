Matchupz Demo

Demonstration of the Matchupz application in action.

As part of this project, I contributed to a football analysis tool focused on player tracking and performance evaluation. Using advanced computer vision techniques, I implemented features like object detection and tracking with YOLO11s and Byte Tracker, field keypoint detection with YOLO11n, and real-world position mapping via homography. I also developed player club assignment through jersey color clustering, dynamic Voronoi diagrams for spatial influence, ball possession calculations with grace periods, and speed estimation with real-world scaling and smoothing. This tool processes video footage to provide detailed insights into gameplay dynamics, enhancing performance analysis for teams and players.
